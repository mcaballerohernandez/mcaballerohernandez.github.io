ABOUT

My name is Maria Del Carmen Caballero Hernandez, I was born in Guanajuato, Mexico. I graduated highschool in Jan 2019 and proceeded the year going straight into the workforce. After a year of saving I registered for the Dental Assistant program at NC Dental U. Which I then graduated with a certification in Radiology, Nirtous, and Dental Assistant I.

 

I did my externship at East Carolina University, rotating through oral surgery, pediatrics, and general. I followed through my DAI by getting half of my 3000 hours at Piedmont Health Services Community Health Center, and the other half at University Dental Associates. I am currently a Dental Assistant II.
